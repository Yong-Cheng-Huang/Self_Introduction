College Experience

How did I feel on my first day of college?

The first day of college is always a mix of emotions. Excitement, nervousness, and a little bit of fear are all common feelings for new students. Personally, I remember feeling a combination of excitement and anxiety on my first day of college. I was excited to start this new chapter of my life, meet new people, and explore different academic interests. At the same time, I was nervous about adjusting to a new environment, managing a more demanding workload, and fitting in with my peers. Looking back, I realized that the first day of college is just the beginning of a long and rewarding journey, full of challenges and opportunities for growth.

What are the differences between college and High school?

  • College courses are more focused on independent learning, while high school courses tend to be more structured and teacher-led.
  • College classes are often larger and more diverse, with students from a variety of backgrounds and experiences.
  • College offers more opportunities for exploration and self-discovery, from extracurricular activities to study abroad programs, and encourages students to think critically about their goals and values.
